AI Ping Pong Game







Instructions

1. Press the play button to start the game



2. Move your mouse wherever you want the paddle to go



3. If you don't hit the ball then the computer will score a point



4. Once the computer or yourself reaches four points the game will end



5. If you progress onto the next game without letting the computer score a point, the speed will increase.



That's all. Have fun playing the game!
